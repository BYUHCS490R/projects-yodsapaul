About me

My name is Yosapol Rongthong, I am from Thailand, but I grew up in Philippines, which is Hilongos, Leyte. When I was younger, my family were in poverty. We used to eat rice and coffee as breakfast, so I learned a lot about appreciation in food. I also definitely had a good time there.

I was assigned to serve in Micronesia mission as a full-time missionary. I faced crazy incidents and also spiritual experiences, my mission was strange. Fortunately, I had a chance to open an area and be a branch president. It was the hardest thing for me because I never was in a leadership, or in charge of anything. I was nervous at first so I asked the senior couples that have been helping us in our zone. Their names were Elder & Sister Feller. Elder Feller said something to me that struck into my heart until now. He said

"We are God's god"

-Elder Feller
